## About

**Moment** is a personal visual memory archive project. It acts as a digital space dedicated to curating and presenting life's moments. With a focus on atmosphere and aesthetic, it offers a beautifully crafted experience for preserving and reflecting on memories.

## Features

- 🎞️ **Visual Archive:** A curated collection of personal moments and memories.
- 🎨 **Minimalist Design:** A clean, sleek, and atmospheric UI that puts content first.
- 🚀 **Smooth Interactions:** Fluid transitions and animations to enhance the experience.
- 📱 **Responsive:** Designed to look great across devices.
